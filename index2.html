Agridera Fresh Market Orange

Oranges are a juicy and aromatic citrus fruit that grows on trees in tropical and subtropical regions of the world. This fruit is widely used in cooking and nutrition due to its taste, rich vitamin composition and beneficial properties.

Oranges are high in vitamin C, which not only helps boost the immune system, but is also a powerful antioxidant that protects cells from damage. In addition, they contain vitamin A, potassium, calcium, magnesium, and folic acid, which are essential for healthy bones, heart, nervous system, and other organs.

Oranges can be consumed in a variety of forms: fresh, juiced, canned, or dried. They can be added to salads, pastries, ice cream and other desserts, as well as used to make sauces and marinades.

Oranges are also used in cosmetics for their skin benefits.

Greenhouse Orange Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets.For single and cluster picks, round fruit shape and resistances of TYLCV, TSWV, Nematodes and FORL (Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. radicis-lycopersici). Some hybrids may include more resistances, such as Cladosporium and Leveillula Taurica (Lv).

Open-field Indeterminate Oranges

This breeding is for Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The main focus is on large round shape fruits (200 grams plus); and resistances of TYLCV, TSWV, Nematodes and FORL. In the near future, F3 (Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. lycopersici) will be included as basic resistance in our cultivars.

Open-field Bush Type Oranges

This breeding is for Mediterranean markets.For single and cluster picks, round fruit shape and resistances of TYLCV, TSWV, Nematodes and FORL (Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. radicis-lycopersici). Some hybrids may include more resistances, such as Cladosporium and Leveillula Taurica (Lv).

Orange blossom oil is used in aromatherapy as it is believed to help relieve stress and improve mood.
